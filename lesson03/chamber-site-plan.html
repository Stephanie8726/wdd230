Site Plan

Site Name

Newtown Business Chamber

🤝Welcome to the Newtown Business Chamber, dedicated to fostering economic growth and community development.

🏚️This name represents a city in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose

📌The Mactan Newtown Chamber of Commerce strives to be a driving force in the economic growth and prosperity of the Mactan region. Our purpose is to foster a thriving business community by providing essential services, supporting local development initiatives, and advocating for policies that contribute to the sustainable advancement of businesses in the area.

📌Creating a collaborative platform for businesses in Mactan to network, share resources, and collectively contribute to the community's progress.

Scenarios

✔️1. What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?

✔️2. Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?

✔️3. What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

🌈Primary Color (#1e6091): This color will be used for headings and important elements to grab attention.

🌈Secondary Color (#ffb6c1): This will be used for borders, accents, and to highlight secondary information.

🌈Tertiary Color (#98fb98): And this color will be for background elements and to create a sense of freshness and positivity.

Typography

✍Additional Font: 'Lora', serif;
