Our Mission


At Dream Wedding, our mission is to create unforgettable wedding experiences for couples by providing exceptional planning services and attention to detail. We strive to exceed our clients' expectations and make their special day truly magical.


Our Story


Established in 2015 by Tiffany and Drich Johnson, Dream Wedding began as a cozy family venture focusing on wedding planning services. Driven by their love for crafting unforgettable memories, the Johnsons aimed to provide couples with a seamless and enchanting wedding journey. Since then, Dream Wedding has evolved into a respected wedding planning agency, celebrated for its tailored service and meticulous attention to detail. Our team of seasoned experts is committed to bringing your dream wedding to life.
