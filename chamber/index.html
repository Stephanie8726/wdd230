Multisports Expo

Explore a world of fitness and vitality at the Multisports Expo at the Mactan Newtown Sales & Information Center, where sports enthusiasts and professionals come together to celebrate the diverse realm of multisport activities. This event offers a unique opportunity to discover the latest trends, cutting-edge equipment, and expert insights in various sports disciplines. From exhilarating demonstrations to interactive exhibits, the Multisports Expo is a dynamic gathering that caters to athletes of all levels. Immerse yourself in the excitement of the Multisports Expo and elevate your passion for a healthy and active lifestyle.
